RHETORICA

Your Voice, Your Opinion

Isn't it a privilege indeed to gain broad, multi-faceted knowledge cutting across several disciplines outside the learner's usual academic subjects? If your answer is YES, then the RHETORICA is the opportunity to grab. This is your one stop destination for anything from trying to escape reality, to developing effective speech composition and delivery. Hop on as we give wings to our minds and flight to imagination! RHETORICA is a Media-Based Event that tests your ability to speak about issues that are of significance in this generation, your ability to make changes in the society we live in and also your ability to defend your stance against others.
